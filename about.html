I'm Nishat, a Brand and Logo Designer based in Bangladesh​​​​​​​

__

I am Nishat Tasnima, an independent graphics designer specializing in branding identity & packaging. Most of the time you will find me doing work for fashion, beauty & lifestyle brands. I do pattern design for apparel and that's how my interest in fashion brands has developed & I am always curious about the new trend in the fashion industry. i love trying out skincare brands that are carefully crafted and healthy for my skin, so I do a lot of research before trying out skincare products & this helps me to come up with new ideas for beauty & lifestyle branding.

Besides branding, I love making custom fonts & monograms for my clients. I am a typography lover. You will see me doing typography exploration most of the time on here. (install link)

Branding is all about building emotional connections with your customers through your unique brand story. I believe in making designs that are visually aesthetic and thoughtful with a creative touch that will connect you with your ideal clients.
