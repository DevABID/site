Auda

Auda is a modern skincare brand based in LA. They believe in skin comfortability, and
that's why they have brings their signature skincare label in the form of
facial foam cleanser.

The challenge

Make the brand identity, naming, and packaging vibe traditional and organic. Choosing
the perfect name and logotype which gives you an organic hand lettering feel was a
big challenge! To stand out in the market, it would be best if you were unique!

Auda is an old french word that means comfortable, and no other name can match
the Auda skincare cosmetic brand's vision.
